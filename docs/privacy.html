Privacy Policy: Google Calendar Callback Extension

This Privacy Policy document outlines how the Google Calendar Callback extension ("the Extension") handles user information.

Information Collection

Calendar Data: The Extension accesses your Google Calendar events through the Google Calendar API to identify upcoming meetings with Google Meet links. This data is only processed on your device and is used solely to display timely notifications for your meetings.

Email Address: Your email address is accessed for display purposes within the Extension interface to confirm which Google account is connected. This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any third parties.

Preferences: The Extension stores your preferences (such as selected ringtone, notification timing, and auto-join setting) in your browser's local storage. These settings remain on your device and are not shared externally.

Data Storage

All data processed by the Extension, including:

  • Authentication tokens
  • Calendar event data
  • User preferences
  • Error logs

is stored locally on your device using the Chrome Storage API. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts solely with Google APIs (specifically Calendar and Identity) to function. Your interaction with these services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions for the stated purposes:

  • identity and identity.email: To authenticate with Google and display your email within the Extension.
  • storage: To save your preferences and authentication tokens locally.
  • alarms: To schedule regular checks for upcoming meetings.
  • notifications: To display meeting notifications.
  • tabs and windows: To display the incoming call overlay and open meeting links.
  • Host permissions for Google APIs: To access your calendar data with your permission.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date below. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
